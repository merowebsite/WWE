THEN NOW FOREVER TOGETHER

WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) was founded on January of 1953 by Vince McMahon, Linda McMahon and Jess McMahon. It was rebranded as WWF (Worldwide Wrestling Federation) on 21st February 1980. WWF was renamed as WWE after losing a lawsuit with World Wildlife Fund over WWF trademark. The CEO of the company is Stephanie McMahon (Interim). The president is Nick Khan. WWE is a global entertainment company and its products are live events, streaming network services, television. WWE has also branched out into films and American football (XFL). In 2021 WWE had its first billion dollar year, collecting the revenue of $1.095 billion.
